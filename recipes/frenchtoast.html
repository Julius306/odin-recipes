French Toast Recipe

Description

With this easy recipe you can create really tasty French Toasts!

It won't take too much time and ressources to make!

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • (Optional) Nutella

Steps

  1. Mix Butter Eggs and Milk
  2. Put the toast in it, 60 seconds each side
  3. Fry the toast in a pan
  4. (Optional) Add Nutella
