Lasagna Recipe

Description

With this easy recipe you can create a really tasty lasagna!

It won't take too much time and ressources to make!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Bake the lasagna
